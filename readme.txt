With this plugin you had the power to easily place your banners in any position  you want to on your blog, you could also track your Affiliate banners, find out how good they really are doing.


== Description ==

Normally adding images will need you to edit your wordpress theme, mess with lots of codes and even then result will not even satisfactory. But what if you had the power to easily place your image in any position? What if you can put an affiliate link on your each uploaded image? What if we say, you could also track your uploaded images; find out how good they really are doing and even your image will SEO friendly? What if all of this could be done with one powerful and easy plugin? That is where Affiliate Image Tracker comes into play. Adding, controlling, and tracking the images on your blog more easily..

note : your image will be displayed randomly one at a time from a group of image. 


**Features**

* "No" loading time. the images are loaded dynamically one at a time from large group of image uploaded.
* Can be integrated in template files, posts and pages.
* No limitation in size at the time of uploading.
* Can fix image height only or width only or both while displaying image or you can also Leave the image as it is.
* Affiliate link Open in new window option
* and many more. 


== Installation ==

1. Download "Affiliate Image Tracker" plugin.
1. Open /wp-content/plugins folder at your web server.
1. Upload the folder "Affiliate Image Tracker" there.
1. Goto Plugins page in your wordpress back office.
1. Activate "Affiliate Image Tracker" plugin.
1. Go to Settings >> Affiliate Image Tracker for modifying the settings.

Note: This plugin will require one time free registration.

== How to use it ==

* Go to Settings >> Affiliate Image Tracker >> Add new affiliate image
* Fill necesseary fileds and upload image.
* Adjust Advance Image Option before displaying images.
* Go on widgets to display your Affiliate image on siderbar.
* use Post Tag to display Affiliate image  on post and page.
* use template Tag to display anywhere on your site.
